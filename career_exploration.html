Explore Your Career Pathways

Career exploration is a vital step in shaping your professional journey. It involves researching various industries, roles, and companies to find the best fit for your skills, interests, and values. By understanding the landscape of potential careers, you can make informed decisions about your future and set meaningful goals.

This page provides resources and strategies to help you navigate the career exploration process effectively.

Use AI Prompts to find Career Paths that suit you Best

Not sure what career path suits you best? AI can suggest potential careers, provide insights on job market trends, and help you identify relevant skills for different industries. The key to using AI effectively is to explore multiple options, ask targeted questions about specific fields, and use AI-generated insights as a starting point for deeper research.

  • Example Prompt: “My values are [insert values], some of my top skills are [insert skills], and I have interests in [insert interests]. What careers align with my values? What careers align with my skills? What careers align with my interests?”
  • Example prompt: “I want to live in [insert location], and I am interested in a career in [insert industry]. My values are [insert values]. What are some organizations I could consider applying to?”
  • Example prompt: “I have skills in [insert skills]. I am most interested in [insert interests]. My career goal is to [insert goal]. I have already started applying to some jobs with titles such as [insert titles]. Please create a list of other types of jobs/internships that align with my skills, interests, and goals.”
